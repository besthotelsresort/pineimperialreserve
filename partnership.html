Partner with MapleLeaf Retreats

We work with hotels, resorts, tourism boards and travel brands that share our commitment to honest, guest-first hospitality.

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Each year more than a million travellers read MapleLeaf Retreats to decide where to stay in Canada. That trust is our most valuable asset, and it shapes every partnership we enter.

Ways to work with us

Affiliate & referral partnerships

If your property or booking platform runs an affiliate program, we can send high-intent, well-informed travellers directly to your reservations team through our "Reserve a room" enquiries and contextual links. We're paid on performance, so our incentives are aligned with sending you guests who actually want to stay.

Destination & tourism collaborations

We partner with tourism boards and regional marketing organisations on editorial travel guides and destination features that help travellers discover lesser-known stays. These collaborations are always clearly labelled.

Data & syndication

Selected partners license our review summaries and rating data for use in their own channels, under agreements that preserve our editorial independence.

What partnership does not buy

A commercial relationship with MapleLeaf Retreats never includes a guaranteed rating, a favourable review, removal of criticism, or a ranking position. Our editorial team operates independently of our commercial team, and a partnership does not make us your official representative. Please read our Affiliate Disclosure.

Our standards for partners

  • Accurate representation of facilities and accessibility;
  • Fair and clearly communicated cancellation and refund policies;
  • Responsive handling of the reservation enquiries we send;
  • A genuine commitment to guest welfare and safety.
